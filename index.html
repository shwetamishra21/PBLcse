Article

Neighbourhood Community App

"A comprehensive Android application that brings neighbourhood communities together on a single, structured platform, facilitating community interaction, local announcements, and service coordination with real-time updates and role-based access control."

Problem Statement

Modern neighbourhood communities lack a centralized digital platform for communication, service requests, and community management. Residents struggle to find local service providers, coordinate with neighbours, and stay updated with community announcements, leading to fragmented communication and inefficient service delivery.

Market Research

Analyzed existing platforms like Nextdoor and WhatsApp groups. Found gaps in structured service management, role-based access control, and integrated real-time communication for neighbourhood communities.

Innovation

Article provides a unified platform with multi-role system (Members, Service Providers, Admins), real-time request tracking, integrated messaging, and premium Material 3 UI design, offering a complete ecosystem for neighbourhood management.

System Architecture

Frontend

Kotlin • Jetpack Compose • Material 3 • Navigation Compose • Coil Image Loading • MVVM Architecture
